Veggie Pasta

Home

Description

This is a healthy, tasty and slightly spicy veggie pasta! The pasta used in this recipe is high protein, which keeps the calories low and keeps the protein high. The meal is approximately 417 calories with 54 grams of protein.

You can meal prep this, I do it often. The pasta can be stored up to 4 days in a sealed container in the fridge. You don't feel like you are missing out on meat which makes it even better!

Ingredients

  • 375g Smart pasta protein spaghetti by vetta
  • 400g Italian tomato passata
  • 500g Zucchini
  • 200g Sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups Baby spinach
  • 1 Medium onion
  • 3 cloves garlic crushed
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon italian herbs

Steps

  1. Chop up onion and peel the zucchini to create strips. Slice these strips to make zucchini spaghetti.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add protein spaghetti and cook for 12 minutes. Drain pasta and save a cup of pasta water.
  3. Heat a large deep pan over medium heat and add chopped onion. Cook for 4-5 minutes or until softened. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add mushrooms with onion and garlic to cook for another 4-5 minutes. Add zucchini spaghetti and cook for 2 minutes. Add spinach and stir until wilted.
  5. Pour in passata and add all spices. Add one cup of pasta water and stir. Let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat sauce evenly.
  7. Serve with parmesan cheese or put in container in fridge for later!
