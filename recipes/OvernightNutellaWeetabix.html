Overnight Nutella Weetabix

Home

Description

This is a yummy sweet breakfast that is low calorie and high in protein. The calories for this breakfast is 308 calories with 35g of protein! It tastes like a cheesecake drizzled with Nutella.

This is an easy 5 minute breakfast that can be meal prepped for the week! Different yoghurt can be used if preffered, and toppings can be changed to bananas, raspberries or blueberries.

Ingredients

  • 2 Weet-bix biscuits
  • 35ml sugar free maple syrup
  • 160g low sugar high protein yoghurt
  • 15g Nutella
  • 50g strawberries

Steps

  1. Crush Weet-bix in a bowl and mix with sugar free maple syrup until it makes a cheesecake base.
  2. Pour yoghurt on top of crushed Weet-bix.
  3. Slice the strawberries and place on top of yoghurt.
  4. Melt Nutella and drizzle on top of strawberries. Enjoy now or put in fride for later!
