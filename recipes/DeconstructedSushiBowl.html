Deconstructed Sushi Bowl

Home

Description

This Deconstructed sushi bowl is easy, quick and low calorie! It is packed with 36g of protein and 19.5g of healthy fats whilst being only 465 calories.

This can be made for lunch or dinner and keeps you full for a very long time! If you meal prep this is a great choice. You can also pre-cook a huge batch of rice to meal prep or just pull out of the freezer and whack in the microwave for 2 minutes to warm up for the meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 can of springwater tuna
  • 1 can of tuna in chilli oil (if you don't like chilli can swap for springwater)
  • Soy sauce
  • 50% reduced fat Kewpie Mayonnaise
  • 1 or 2 sheets of seaweed

Steps

  1. Cook the white rice. You can cook a big batch and put other rice in freezer.
  2. Wash and chop up the cucumber.
  3. Cut avocado.
  4. Combine cucumber, avocado, rice and tuna in bowl.
  5. Put one tablespoon (15g) of Soy Sauce and one tablespoon (15g) of Mayonnaise.
  6. Serve with sheets of seaweed and enjoy!
